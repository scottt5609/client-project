How to slam dunk

Best trick shots and dunks

Technique

  • Get in the right mental space
  • Start at the 3 point line
  • Run to the basket
  • Do a 2 step jump as shown above
  • Jump off both feet
  • Dunk the ball

Get stronger

  • Jump a lot of times
  • Play in pickup basketball games
  • Lifting weights can help
  • Practice hitting a punching bag
  • Try to dunk a lot

How to practice

  • Find a basket you can lower
  • Do fancy dunks on it
  • Raise up the basket
  • Try to dunk higher
  • Dunk it until you're too tired
